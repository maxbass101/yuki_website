Yuki:
Carpathian Saga
Project

The game tells about a girl from Japan who comes with her mother to the heart of the Carpathian Mountains and meets her grandfather. Her grandfather turns out to be a local herbalist and forest keeper, but he is already old, and the threat looming over the Carpathians is deforestation.

Unexpectedly, the girl gets into a night adventure, meets forest characters who need help and, without the right to choose, takes on a mission - to save the forest.

On his way, the player will meet both friends and enemies, many unexpected situations in which he will have to make a choice.

Passing the levels, the player collects melodies, which can then be listened to in the player available in the menu. In the process of passing the player learns about the dangers that deforestation hides. The planet is our home and we decide how it will be.
